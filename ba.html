Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) program at Suvidya Degree College offers a comprehensive and flexible curriculum designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the humanities and social sciences. Our BA program encourages critical thinking, creativity, and a deep understanding of various cultural, historical, and societal contexts.

Key Features

  • Diverse Subjects: Explore a wide range of subjects including Literature, History, Sociology, Political Science, Psychology, and more.
  • Experienced Faculty: Learn from knowledgeable and passionate educators dedicated to your academic and personal growth.
  • Holistic Development: Participate in various extracurricular activities, workshops, and seminars to enhance your overall development.
  • Career Opportunities: Graduates can pursue careers in education, public service, media, research, and many other fields.

Course Structure

Our BA program is structured to provide a balanced mix of core subjects and electives, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests and career goals. The course is spread over three years, divided into six semesters.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification: Completion of Higher Secondary Education (10+2) or equivalent from a recognized board.

Minimum Marks: A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the qualifying examination (45% for reserved category students as per government norms).

Subject Requirements: No specific subject requirements; students from any stream can apply.

Age Limit: No age limit for admission to the BA program.
