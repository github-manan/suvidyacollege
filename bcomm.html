Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Comm.) program at Suvidya Degree College provides a thorough understanding of commerce and business principles. Our BCom program equips students with essential skills in accounting, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship, preparing them for a successful career in the dynamic world of business.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Covering subjects such as Accounting, Business Law, Economics, Management, and Taxation.
  • Experienced Faculty: Benefit from the expertise and industry experience of our dedicated faculty members.
  • Practical Exposure: Gain hands-on experience through internships, workshops, and real-world business projects.
  • Career Prospects: Graduates can pursue careers in accounting, finance, marketing, banking, and more.

Course Structure

The BCom program is structured to provide a solid foundation in commerce subjects, complemented by elective courses to tailor your education to your career goals. The course spans three years, divided into six semesters.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification: Completion of Higher Secondary Education (10+2) or equivalent from a recognized board.

Minimum Marks: A minimum aggregate score of 50% in the qualifying examination (45% for reserved category students as per government norms).

Subject Requirements: Preference for students with a background in Commerce, but students from any stream can apply.

Age Limit: No age limit for admission to the BCom program.
